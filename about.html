About BN Media Films

BN Media Films is a film production company focused on telling teenage and young adult stories. Our work is rooted in the realities of youth identity, ambition, relationships, peer pressure, dreams, mistakes, and the quiet moments that shape who young people become. We believe these stories matter, and we are intentional about telling them with honesty, emotional depth, and cultural relevance.

Over the years, we have built a body of work that reflects this focus. Our productions include Like Filtered Water, Breakout, Class of Secrets, Not Perfect Date, and Hope Alive. Each project explores different stages of youth and young adulthood, from school life and self-discovery to love, ambition, consequences, and the pressure to survive and succeed.

Through our films and series, we are committed to creating stories that young audiences can see themselves in, stories that feel familiar, truthful, and emotionally grounded. Whether it is a coming-of-age drama, a dance-driven series, a mystery, or a romantic short film, our goal remains the same: to tell authentic stories that connect, resonate, and stay with the audience long after the screen goes dark.
