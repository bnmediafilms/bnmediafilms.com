Who We Are

BN Media Films is a film production company focused on telling teenage and young adult stories. Our work is rooted in the realities of youth identity, ambition, relationships, peer pressure, dreams, mistakes, and the quiet moments that shape who young people become.

Over the years, we have built a body of work that reflects this focus. Our productions include In Like Filtered Water, Breakout, Class of Secrets, Not Perfect Date, and Hope Alive. Each project explores different stages of youth and young adulthood.

Through our films and series, we are committed to creating stories that young audiences can see themselves in — stories that feel familiar, truthful, and emotionally grounded.
