BN Media Films Announces Strategic Partnership with Patmos Studios

BN Media Films · Latest News

BN Media Films is thrilled to announce a strategic partnership with Patmos Studios, a leading media production company based in Awka, Anambra State. This collaboration marks a significant step in our commitment to delivering high-quality storytelling and productions that resonate with audiences nationwide.

As part of this partnership, we recently opened auditions for our upcoming web series, Sure Banker, which explores the impact of betting and gambling culture in modern society. We were amazed by the overwhelming response, receiving over 3,700 audition entries, demonstrating the immense talent and passion of aspiring actors across Nigeria.

Through this partnership, BN Media Films and Patmos Studios aim to combine creative storytelling with top-notch production expertise, bringing fresh, engaging content to our growing audience.
